Hey, I'm Cheviti Sanjay Kumar, a freelance photographer based in Laxmapur, Medchal, Hyderabad. What started 6 years ago with just a mobile phone and curiosity, grew into a deep passion — and over the last 3–4 years, a DSLR became my creative companion.

A fresh Computer Science graduate (B.Tech 2026) and Diploma holder (2020–23), I also served as one of the official photographers of my college's cultural club — where every event was a new story waiting to be framed. I specialise in portrait, wedding and wildlife photography, with a self-taught eye for composition and a belief that the best shot is always the one you feel before you click.

"Every frame tells a story — I just find the right moment to tell it."
